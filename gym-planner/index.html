How to Use This App

  • Click the week's date to pick a new week
  • Drag & Drop exercises from the sidebar library onto any day in the calendar
  • Double-click on a workout card in the calendar to delete it
  • Hold Ctrl/Cmd and drag a workout to copy it to another day
  • Click on a day's header (e.g., "MONDAY") to zoom in for a focused view
  • In the maximized day view, click the download icon to save that day as a portable HTML file
